# Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective date:** July 8, 2026  
**Applies to:** All use of LMX Cloud (`lmxcloud.io`)

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") supplements our [Terms of Service](./terms-of-service.md). Capitalized terms not defined here have the meanings in the Terms.

## 1. Lawful use only

You may not use the Service to:

- Violate any applicable law or regulation
- Generate, store, or distribute illegal content
- Facilitate fraud, malware, phishing, or unauthorized access to systems
- Harass, threaten, or harm others
- Infringe intellectual property or privacy rights of others
- Circumvent safety filters or provider policies in ways that violate law or third-party terms

## 2. Prohibited content and activities

Examples of prohibited use include (non-exhaustive):

- Child sexual abuse material or exploitation
- Non-consensual intimate imagery
- Instructions for weapons, violence, or serious harm where unlawful
- Spam or unsolicited bulk messaging at scale through our API
- Cryptocurrency mining or unrelated compute abuse through inference endpoints
- Attempting to extract training data or probe models for vulnerabilities in violation of provider rules

You are responsible for outputs generated from your prompts. Review outputs before publication or automated action.

## 3. API keys and accounts

- Do not share, publish, or resell API keys.
- Do not use another user's key without permission.
- Rotate keys if exposed. We may revoke keys that appear compromised.
- Do not create accounts or wallets to evade rate limits, bans, or payment obligations.

## 4. Rate limits and fair use

We enforce rate limits per key, IP, and (for x402) per payer wallet. Excessive automated traffic that degrades the Service for others may be throttled or blocked. Beta capacity is limited; we do not guarantee unlimited throughput.

## 5. Autonomous agents and x402

Agents calling the Service without a human operator must still comply with this AUP. Payment via x402 or USDC does not exempt callers from these rules. We may block wallets, IPs, or payment payloads associated with abuse.

## 6. Enforcement

We may investigate suspected violations, suspend or terminate access, withhold credits, cancel verified payments where the protocol allows, and cooperate with law enforcement when required. We are not obligated to monitor all content but may use automated and manual review for abuse prevention.

## 7. Reporting abuse

Report violations to **support@lmxcloud.io** with subject `Abuse report`. Include timestamps, wallet addresses or API key IDs (never send full secrets), transaction hashes, and a description of the issue.

## 8. Changes

We may update this AUP. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance. The current version is always published at `/legal/acceptable-use`.
